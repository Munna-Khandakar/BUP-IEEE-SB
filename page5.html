Industrial Tour To Robi

IEEE BUP SB has successfully organized the very first industrial visit to the Robi Axiata Head office on 26 September, 2019. The aim of the visit was to introduce the participants to the professional environment of the telecommunication sector.
Three faculty members from the Department of ICT- Assistant Professor Dr. S M Salim Reza, Lecturer Md. Maynul Islam and Lecturer Mahdia Tahsin joined 55 students on the tour to Robi Head Office. The participants were from the third year and final year. Branch Counselor of IEEE BUP SB, Md Maynul Islam guided 55 students to this visit.
In three interactive sessions, experts from Robi discussed how the company manages Cyber Security and executes Data Mining and Software Development. A visit to the SOC (Service Operation Center) of Robi was also arranged in this industrial visit. Experts briefed the participants about actually how the works goes on in the company. Students were very enthusiastic to learn how theoretical knowledge came to use in a practical way.
At the end of the sessions, Robi Axiata Limited thanked the faculty members and the participants and presented small gifts for the faculty members. IEEE BUP SB handed over a token of appreciation to the representatives of Robi Axiata Limited for arranging the visit.
