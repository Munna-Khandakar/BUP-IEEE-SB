About us

IEEE BUP SB WIE Affinity Group aspires to promote women engineers and enthusiasts to follow their academic interests and build a career in engineering. It started its journey from November 9, 2019 [Officially] to provide a platform to advance women in technology, embrace the efforts of every gender and support them to reach their potential in technical, research, entrepreneurship and other non-technical sectors. Starting with only 6 members, the membership growth has multiplied into 25 members in only one year. To improve the growth of membership development and retention, the Affinity holds regular skill development workshops exclusively for its members.
We establish the brand value of IEEE by engaging people and educating them to show how they can be part of the solutions to some of the toughest challenges of becoming an engineer and to grow as an individual. IEEE BUP SB WIE Affinity Group raises the profiles of our remarkable female engineers and encourages those who aspire to become engineers. We believe that by narrowing the gender gap in the fields of Engineering and technology we can transform the future of humanity. And this is how we can move forward to a better world where the skill of people will define them, not their gender.
