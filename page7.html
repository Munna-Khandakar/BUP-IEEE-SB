Benefits of IEEE Membership

A session on "Benefits of IEEE Membership" was held on 5th November. The session was conducted by the Branch Chair, IEEE BUP SB, Md. Jaber Al Nahian. Along with 20 IEEE members, 50 students were present in the session. A brief speech on the importance of becoming an IEEE member was given.
